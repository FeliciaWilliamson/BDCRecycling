Why Recycling Is Important

  • One ton of recycled cardboard saves 46 gallons of oil.
  • 80% of items in landfills could be recycled
  • 9 out of 10 people said they would recycle if it was easier
  • The United States throws away $11.4 billion worth of recyclable containers and packaging every year.

These are only a few reason you should recycle, the benefits of recycling are abundant. Since we simplify the process there is no readon not to choose a healthy Earth.
