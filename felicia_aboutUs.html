About Us

When you saw BDC in our name did you wonder what it stood for? BDC isn't random letters, the B stands for Brayden, my oldest son.The D stands for Dawnica my daughter, and C for Colton my youngest son. BDC Recycling started out as a business that would give my kids some meaning. They love this Earth that the Lord has given us and hate seeing litter and the increasing numerous landfills. This business is us as a family doing our part and taking a stand.

Mission

We can help you create a better future for those you love. We aim to help families who don't recycle because they are short on time,or have no transportion method to the center. We provide the bins, and pick up your recyclable material. We make it easy so you can do your part to protect your families future.
